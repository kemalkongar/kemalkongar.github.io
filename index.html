Work

While I'm always after finding new and interesting software projects, I'm also passionate about writing and editing articles.

My latest software project is internetstockpicks.com. It's a website that scrapes Reddit for financial data. After processing said data, it creates a popularity ranking for companies listed in the NASDAQ and the NYSE. The code can be found on my GitHub along with my other projects.

As an Associate Editor at the McGill Pre-law Review and the Primary Editor of Law and Theory at the Contemporary Review of Genocide and Political Violence, I enjoy working with writers to turn their ideas into published articles. I'm personally passionate about international relations and my articles on the topic can be found on crgreview.com.
